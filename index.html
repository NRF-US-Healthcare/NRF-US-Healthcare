Link to Webinars Folder in Filesite.

Note: You must have Outlook open for this link to work. After clicking, a pop up menu will appear asking if you want to "Open IwlProto Application." This is normal, just click open.
This link directs your current Outlook window to a filesite folder containing all Webinar presentations. If you would like it to open in a separate window:
1. Locate the Outlook icon in your taskbar at bottom of screen.
2. Right-click on it.
3. Click on menu option called Outlook 2013.
This will open a new Outlook window that will be changed when you click link above.
